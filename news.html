Second phase of Kg Melayu Subang flood mitigation project set for 2023

The flood mitigation project involving Sungai Pelempas near Kampung Melayu Subang is midway through, with phase one completed.The appointment of consultant and awarding of tender for phase two is scheduled for next year (2023).“The Drainage and Irrigation Department (DID) is carrying out the flood mitigation project over two phases to address flooding in Kampung Melayu Subang, Shah Alam,” said Selangor infrastructure and public amenities, agriculture modernisation and agro-based industry committee chairman Izham Hashim.

HARIAN METRO

Khairy visited flood victims in the field.

Kuala Lumpur: Yesterday's floods at Taman Setia Warisan, Kampung Melayu Subang, Selangor, drew the attention of Sungai Buloh parliamentary candidate Khairy Jamaluddin, who came to the pitch to listen to residents' complaints. Khairy also offered to send a crew to clean the homes of the locals after the floodwaters receded. "So we'll work on it, and the Department of Irrigation and Drainage (JPS) has also recommended raising the wall or extending the tyre that's been built to the bottom so that the water doesn't overflow." "I hope everyone here is patient," he remarked.

RMAF comes to aid of Kg Melayu Subang

PETALING JAYA: Some 35 families and households from Kampung Melayu Subang were all smiles yesterday after receiving aid in the form of essential goods as well as clothes to celebrate Hari Raya Aidilfitri this weekend.The aid distribution drive was jointly organised by Bakti Malaysia Emergency Rescue and Medical Volunteers Team (PSPPKPBM), Royal Malaysian Air Force (RMAF) personnel based in Subang Air Force Base and the Village Community Management Council (MPKK) of Kampung Melayu Subang.
